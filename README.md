I develop open-source packages indexed on the Comprehensive R Archive Network (CRAN) to resolve the ["Continental Fallacy"](https://rweekly.org/2026-W12.html#RinAcademia) - the systemic bias that treats our oceans as a barrier than a medium of connectivity in spatial modeling. As a Statistics major and public official, my work is designed to address the localized needs of archipelagic life.
