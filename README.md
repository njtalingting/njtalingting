I develop open-source packages indexed on CRAN to resolve the ["Continental Fallacy"](https://rweekly.org/2026-W12.html#RinAcademia) - the systemic bias that treats our oceans as a barrier than a medium of connectivity in spatial modeling. As a Statistics major and public official, my work is designed to address the localized needs of my constitients that "global" models tend to overlook.
